# Appliance

![flowchart_appliance](./images/flowchart-appliance-registration.jpg)

## <ins>Register appliances</ins>
1.	You can register an appliance:
- From the  appliance menu in the sidebar
- On the Appliance Home page
![register_appliance](./images/register-appliance.png)

2.	Click REGISTER after entering the information on the appliance registration. The name value is required; other information is optional.
![register_appliance](./images/register-appliance-new.png)

## <ins>Update Appliance</ins>
1.	Double click on the appliance name you want to update.
2.	Enter all necessary information.
3.	Click **Update**

## <ins>Delete Appliance</ins>
1.	Double click on the appliance name you want to delete.
2.	Click **Delete This Appliance**
